Our Story

Frustrated with how society talks big about saving the environment through essays, art contests, and climate change runs, but still uses plastics, wastes water, and pollutes, I decided it was time for action. This drive brought together a group of like-minded individuals, and that's how ISHARA, our line of eco-friendly products, was born.
